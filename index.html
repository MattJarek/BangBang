Bang Bang Broccoli with Sweet Chili Sauce

This Bang Bang Broccoli recipe is a crispy, spicy, and addictive twist on a veggie favorite. A light tempura-style batter enhanced with soda water ensures extra crunch, while the signature sauce brings creamy heat. Perfect as an appetizer or snack.

Activity Time
Prep Broccoli & Batter15 minutes
Fry Broccoli20 minutes
Make Sauce5 minutes
Assemble & Serve5 minutes
Total Time45 minutes

Crispy Broccoli

The batter is light and crispy, thanks to the addition of soda water. Use cold soda water for best results.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into large florets
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup brown rice flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup cold soda water
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom (optional)
  • 1 tsp ground coriander (optional)
  • 1 tsp ground ginger (optional)
  • Sunflower oil for frying

Procedure

  1. Whisk the eggs and soda water in a bowl until smooth.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the dry ingredients: rice flour, cornstarch, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dredge each broccoli floret in the dry mix, dip in the wet batter, then re-coat in the dry mix.
  4. Heat oil to 350°F and fry florets until golden, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Drain on paper towels.

Bang Bang Sauce

This sauce delivers a creamy, sweet heat that coats the crispy broccoli perfectly.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise (or vegan mayo)
  • 1/4 cup Thai sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tsp sriracha (adjust to taste)

Procedure

  1. Combine mayo, sweet chili sauce, and sriracha in a bowl.
  2. Whisk until smooth and creamy.
  3. Toss the fried broccoli in the sauce or serve it on the side.

Enjoy your crispy Bang Bang Broccoli fresh and hot. Leftovers can be reheated in an oven to restore crunch.
